## 📊 Project Overview

This dashboard transforms raw sales data into actionable insights, allowing stakeholders to monitor profitability, identify top-performing products, and understand regional market trends.

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
*   **Total Sales:** $2.30M
*   **Total Profit:** $286K
*   **Total Quantity Sold:** 38K
*   **Average Discount:** 16%

---

## 📂 Dashboard Pages & Features

### 1. Home / Executive Summary
Provides a high-level snapshot of the business health.
*   **Sales by Region:** Tracks performance across West, East, Central, and South (West leading at $0.76M).
*   **Monthly Trends:** Visualizes seasonality, showing significant growth toward Q4 (November/December).
*   **Category Profitability:** Highlights that **Technology** is the most profitable category ($145K).

### 2. Customer Analysis
Focused on demographics and individual buyer performance.
*   **Segmentation:** Breakdown of sales by Consumer (51%), Corporate (31%), and Home Office (19%).
*   **Top Customers:** Visualizes high-value clients like Sean Miller and Tamara Chand.
*   **Geographic Distribution:** Sales by city and region to identify market penetration.

### 3. Product Analysis
Deep dive into inventory and product-level success.
*   **Top 10 Products:** Identifies the **Canon imageCLASS** copier as the highest sales driver ($62K).
*   **Sub-Category Treemap:** Displays volume for Phones, Chairs, Storage, and more.
*   **Profit vs. Sales:** Compares which products drive revenue versus those that drive actual profit.

### 4. Shipping & Logistics
Analyzes the efficiency of the supply chain.
*   **Ship Mode:** Analysis of Standard Class (dominant at 59%), Second Class, First Class, and Same Day.
*   **Shipping Volume:** Tracks the total count of shipping orders (5,009) and average shipping durations.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
*   **Tool:** Power BI Desktop
*   **Data Modeling:** Star Schema
*   **DAX:** Used for complex measures (Total Profit, YoY Growth, Avg Discount).
*   **Power Query:** For ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) and data cleaning.

---

## 💡 Key Business Insights
*   **West is Best:** The West region is the primary revenue driver, contributing nearly 33% of total sales.
*   **Technology Wins:** Despite lower volume than Office Supplies, the Technology category produces significantly higher profit margins.
*   **Seasonality:** There is a clear "End of Year" spike in orders, suggesting the need for increased inventory and staffing during Q4.
*   **Standard Shipping:** Most customers prefer "Standard Class" shipping, indicating a preference for lower costs over delivery speed.

---

## 🚀 How to View
1. Download the `.pbix` file from this repository.
2. Open the file using [Power BI Desktop](https://powerbi.microsoft.com/desktop/).
3. Use the navigation buttons on the left sidebar to toggle between **Home, Customer, Product, and Shipping** views.
